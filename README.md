###### `§ 01 — THE BRIEF`

# A developer's <br/>quiet manifesto.

I build software the way a tailor cuts cloth — measured, deliberate, with an
eye for the line you do not see. My work lives at the seam between **web**,
**mobile**, and the slow-moving machinery that holds them together.

I care about the pixel and the protocol in equal measure. I read system-design
papers like other people read novels. I am most awake when a problem still
looks like a fog — and I get to walk into it.
